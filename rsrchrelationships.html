Relationships

A supportive social provides emotional support and instrumental suppport It's hard to be comfortable if you have not been raised with a strong sense of self esteem

"A supportive social provides emotional support and instrumental suppport"

Research tells us you will like to be kind of people you like to spend time with and vice versa. If you tend towards negativel, find ways to change your attitude with mindfullness and gratitude. It will all come with practise

Practical Steps

  • Detach from love. Do some soul searching. Do some Eat. Pray. Love.

  • Express appreciation. You should experiment with that.

  • Be the person you want to be.

  • Be wierd. Instead of trying to fit in be different, be yourself.
